Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Katherine Flannery, but I’m known as Kat. I’m currently a junior at CodeRVA Regional High School.

I’ve played music for 7 years now. My musical journey was a long one; when I was younger I would play on and off, but when I turned 10 I picked up rock bass guitar and I’ve been playing nonstop ever since. I remember I wanted to play bass guitar because when my dad bought me a guitar, my wrist was too small to flex around the neck. I grew into it however, because after 4 years of bass guitar I started to teach myself regular guitar. Ever since then, I’ve taught myself keyboard, violin/viola/cello, ukulele, melodica, I can play the drums with some competency, though it isn’t my strong suit admittedly, and I can sing. Music is my life; I don’t know where I’d be without it.

I taught myself how to draw, though I’m still learning. I know how to draw up images in vector programs such as Inkscape. I was a creative writer when I was younger, around the eighth grade, though I haven’t touched it in a while because of my music.
